Visual-To-Text Ideation Canvas · C64 Mode

Draw rectangular layout blocks, pan/zoom the viewport, and export ASCII + JSON + YAML.

Tools

Selection

--

Pan: middle mouse drag OR hold Space + left drag

Zoom: mouse wheel in viewport

Select: click a block (Alt+click cycles overlaps; Shift starts draw on occupied cells)

Shortcuts: D · E · F (focus) · Esc · Del · Ctrl/Cmd+Z · Ctrl+Y

Nudge: Arrow keys (Shift = 5) · Resize: Alt+Arrow (Shift = 5)
